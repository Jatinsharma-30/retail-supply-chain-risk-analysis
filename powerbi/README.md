# Power BI Dashboard

## Dashboard Title

Retail Supply Chain Risk Analysis Dashboard

## Objective

The purpose of this dashboard is to provide a centralized view of inventory performance, sales trends, demand forecasting accuracy, and supply chain risks. It helps stakeholders monitor inventory health, identify potential stock shortages, evaluate sales performance, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard combines inventory, sales, forecasting, pricing, and seasonal performance metrics into a single interactive report.

### Key KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Inventory Value
- Total Units Ordered
- Average Forecast Error
- High Risk Inventory Count

---

## Visualizations

### 1. Sales by Category

**Chart Type:** Clustered Bar Chart

**Purpose:**
- Compare sales performance across product categories.
- Identify top-performing and low-performing categories.

---

### 2. Sales by Region

**Chart Type:** Column Chart

**Purpose:**
- Analyze regional sales distribution.
- Identify regions contributing the highest sales volume.

---

### 3. Inventory Risk Distribution

**Chart Type:** Donut Chart

**Purpose:**
- Visualize the proportion of High Risk and Low Risk inventory.
- Support inventory planning and replenishment decisions.

---

### 4. Sales by Seasonality

**Chart Type:** Column Chart

**Purpose:**
- Evaluate the impact of seasonal patterns on sales.
- Identify peak-performing seasons.

---

### 5. Actual Sales vs Demand Forecast

**Chart Type:** Line Chart

**Purpose:**
- Compare actual sales with forecasted demand.
- Assess forecasting performance over time.

---

### 6. Discount Impact on Sales

**Chart Type:** Column Chart

**Purpose:**
- Measure the effectiveness of discounts on sales volume.
- Understand promotional impact on customer demand.

---

## Interactive Filters (Slicers)

The dashboard includes the following filters:

- Region
- Category
- Year
- Seasonality

Users can dynamically filter the report to perform detailed analysis.

---

## Business Insights Supported

The dashboard helps answer the following business questions:

1. Which categories generate the highest sales?
2. Which regions contribute most to revenue generation?
3. How much inventory is currently at risk?
4. How accurate are demand forecasts?
5. How does seasonality affect sales performance?
6. What is the impact of discounts on product sales?
