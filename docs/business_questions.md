# Business Questions

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze retail inventory, sales performance, demand forecasting accuracy, and external factors affecting product demand. The analysis aims to provide actionable insights that support inventory optimization, sales growth, and data-driven business decisions.

---

## Inventory Analysis

### 1. Which categories currently have the highest inventory levels?

**Business Value:**
Helps identify inventory concentration and potential overstock situations.

### 2. Which products or categories are at risk of stock shortages?

**Business Value:**
Supports timely replenishment decisions and reduces lost sales opportunities.

### 3. What is the total inventory value across categories and regions?

**Business Value:**
Provides visibility into capital tied up in inventory.

---

## Sales Performance Analysis

### 4. Which product categories generate the highest sales volume?

**Business Value:**
Identifies top-performing product segments.

### 5. Which regions contribute the most sales?

**Business Value:**
Helps management understand regional performance differences.

### 6. Which stores demonstrate the strongest sales performance?

**Business Value:**
Highlights high-performing locations and best practices.

---

## Demand Forecast Analysis

### 7. How accurate are demand forecasts compared to actual sales?

**Business Value:**
Evaluates forecasting effectiveness and planning accuracy.

### 8. Which categories have the largest forecast errors?

**Business Value:**
Identifies areas where forecasting models require improvement.

### 9. Are products being over-forecasted or under-forecasted?

**Business Value:**
Supports inventory optimization and demand planning.

---

## Pricing Analysis

### 10. What is the relationship between discounts and units sold?

**Business Value:**
Measures the effectiveness of promotional strategies.

### 11. How does competitor pricing influence sales performance?

**Business Value:**
Supports pricing and competitive positioning decisions.

---

## Seasonal and External Factors Analysis

### 12. How do seasonal patterns affect product demand?

**Business Value:**
Supports seasonal inventory planning.

### 13. How do weather conditions impact sales performance?

**Business Value:**
Identifies external factors affecting customer purchasing behavior.

### 14. What impact do holidays and promotions have on demand?

**Business Value:**
Measures the effectiveness of promotional campaigns.
