# Key Insights

## Inventory Risk

- 3.54% of records were identified as high inventory risk.
- Most products maintain sufficient inventory levels relative to forecasted demand.

## Regional Performance

- East region generated the highest sales volume.
- Sales distribution remained relatively balanced across all regions.

## Seasonality

- Autumn recorded the highest sales.
- Seasonal variation was relatively small.

## Inventory Value

- Furniture represented the highest inventory value.
- Inventory investment was distributed evenly across categories.
